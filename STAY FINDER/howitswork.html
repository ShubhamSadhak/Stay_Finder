How StayFinder Works

Finding a PG doesn’t need to be hard. Here’s how StayFinder makes it quick and hassle-free:

1. Search

Enter your preferred location, budget, and occupancy type to view PGs that match your needs.

2. Compare

Compare different PGs by price, reviews, distance, and amenities to make an informed decision.

3. Book

Schedule a visit or book online. StayFinder offers secure payments and verified listings.
