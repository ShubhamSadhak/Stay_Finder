Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a PG through StayFinder? ▼

Browse available PGs, apply filters, and click "Book Now" or "Schedule Visit" on the listing page. Our support team will assist you in the next steps.

How do I list my property on StayFinder? ▼

Go to the "List Property" page, fill out the form with your PG's details, and submit it. Our team will review and publish your listing within 24–48 hours.

Are the PG listings verified? ▼

Yes, we manually verify every listing via photos, documents, or in-person inspection to ensure authenticity and safety.

Is there any service charge? ▼

StayFinder does not charge users for browsing. A small convenience fee may apply at the time of booking or for premium support services.

Can I edit my property details later? ▼

Yes, property owners can log into their dashboard and edit property details anytime. Changes are reviewed before going live.
