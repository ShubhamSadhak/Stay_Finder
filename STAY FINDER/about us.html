About StayFinder

We are on a mission to simplify the process of finding PG accommodations in India’s bustling cities.

StayFinder was born out of the challenges faced by the relocating students and professionals who had to rely on unverified listings, word-of-mouth, or tedious broker visits to find a decent PG. We wanted to create a transparent, easy-to-use platform that empowers you to compare PG options, read reviews, view amenities, and book online — all in one place.

Whether you're moving for studies, work, or a fresh start, we help you find verified PGs that suit your lifestyle and budget. Ensuring rich filters, secure booking, and responsive support, StayFinder ensures your move-in is stress-free.

Our team is made up of engineers, designers, and local experts who understand the ground realities and digital possibilities. We continuously work to improve the experience and bring in innovations like virtual tours, neighborhood scores, and roommate matching.
