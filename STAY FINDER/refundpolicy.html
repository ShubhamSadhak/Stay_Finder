Refund Policy

At StayFinder, we strive to provide a transparent and fair refund policy for both PG seekers and property owners.

1. Eligibility for Refunds

Refunds are applicable under the following conditions:

  • If a booking is canceled within the free cancellation window (if applicable).
  • If the listed property is found to be fraudulent or non-existent.
  • If a duplicate payment was made due to a technical error.

2. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please email us at refunds@stayfinder.in with your booking ID and reason for the request. Our team will process valid requests within 5–7 business days.

3. Non-Refundable Payments

The following payments are generally non-refundable:

  • Service or convenience fees once booking is confirmed.
  • Premium or featured listing charges once property is live.
  • Late cancellations or no-shows.

4. Mode of Refund

Approved refunds will be processed to the original payment method (credit card, UPI, wallet, etc.). You will be notified by email once the refund is initiated.

5. Contact Support

For any clarification regarding refunds, you can reach us at support@stayfinder.in or call us at +91-6289952219.
